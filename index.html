Abhinav Narayan Harish

I am working as a research fellow at IITGN with Professor Shanmuganathan Raman in the domain of 3D-Computer Vision and Machine Learning. My long term goal is to apply this knowledge in robotics by simulating realistic interactions with a virtual environment.

I recently graduate from Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar with a Major in Electrical Engineering and a Minor in Computer Science. During my undergraduate years, I was fortunate to work with Professor Nitin Khanna on learning based forensics for JPEG images.
